Learning Tips

Get the most from Signify

Watch First

Open the GIF and watch the full movement loop at least 3 times before trying to replicate it yourself.

Mirror Practice

Practice in front of a mirror so you can see your own hand shape and compare it to the demonstration.

Repeat Daily

Even 5 minutes a day of practice will embed signs into muscle memory faster than long occasional sessions.
